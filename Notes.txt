Prompt - A prompt is simply the text you send to a large-language model. 
        It contains Instructions, Input data, Context, Output Indicator.
        
        Eg: Classify the review as positive, neutral or negative
            Review: These headphones arrives  quickly and look great, but the left earcup stopped working 
                    after a week.
            Sentiment: _____

        Types of prompting techniques:
          Zero-shot prompting: Here we directly ask the question.
          Few-shot prompting: We give multiple examples and then asks the question.
          Chain-of-thought: Model has Reasoning capability
